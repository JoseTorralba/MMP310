About Me

The name's Jose Torralba, an aspiring Graphic Designer & Web Developer from New York City.

I am currently attending Borough of Manhattan Community College to earn my Associate's Degree in Multimedia Arts, Graphic Design. I also have an interest on Web Development and eager to learn more about it.

I'm also working as a Supplemental Instructor for MMP 100 at Borough of Manhattan Community College.

On my free time I spend it on learning more Javascript and advanced HTML/CSS/SASS. The thing is, Monster Hunter World recently came out and that's all I've been spending my time on.
