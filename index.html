About Me

The name is Jose Torralba, an aspiring front end web developer from New York City. I currently have an Associate's degree in Multimedia Arts from Borough of Manhattan Community College.

At the moment, I am working as a Supplemental Instructor at BMCC for Multimedia Programming 100 and 210. I assist students with their classwork, assignments, and projects alongside the professor. I try my best to help students better understand the course materials and make it enjoyable.
